Name

GiLi Clean (Limpiecito Cleaning Services LLC DBA GiLi Clean)

A company dedicated to house cleaning, providing our clients the opportunity to use their time to achieve their personal goals.

Domain: www.giliclean.com

Purpose

Provide to the public and potential clients within Utah State, with the information about the company and answer to their questions regarding our services including:

  • Move in and Move Out cleaning
  • Recurring (Standard) Cleaning
  • Full Cleaning
  • One Time Cleaning
  • After Remodeling Cleaning
